How Twofold Spaces Transform Your Furnitures To Make Your Home More Spacious

The Author September 9, 2022 Startups

The global furniture market is projected to grow from USD 493.6 billion in 2021 to USD 720.2 billion by 2028 at a CAGR of 5.5% in the 2021-2028 period. The global impact of covid-19 has been unprecedented and staggering, with the market witnessing a positive demand shock across all regions amid the pandemic. Based on the analysis, the global market exhibited a stellar growth of 10% in 2020. 

Enter Twofold Spaces, a company that creates on-demand, space-saving furniture for the home, workplace, and everywhere in between.

Twofold Spaces

Developed with a deep understanding of the limitations of real estate in an increasingly dense world, they design and manufacture products that revive productivity, flexibility, and functionality in idle areas. Anja Bump is the Founder and CEO of the company. She brings 20 years of experience in operations, manufacturing, and supply chain management to the table. 

The company focuses on reinventing one's space with a lot of innovative ideas which resultantly look beautiful and stylish at the same time. There is a variety of products that the company offers. Diving into the details of the products they have an office plaza, micro plaza, space table, health plaza, etc. 

Plaza Space

Plaza space is their first product in the assembly collection. The thoroughly customizable style maximizes the efficiency of space and ensures to respond to the user's needs by providing an affordable base model for either two or four users with a lot of alternatives. Their two-person Plaza base unit and the four-person unit include the following features: Large, standing height desk, 2 USB and 2 regular outlets, Overhead light, Whiteboard back wall, Acoustic felt wall panels in Twofold orange, Magnetic middle (for hanging a pen holder from it, or notes, or anything one would like), and casters.

Plaza Space system provides a fully loaded unit with the following options for both the two or four-person unit: Comfortable, bench-style seating with a large desk, Bi-fold doors, AV package (includes the display, bi-directional arm, HDMI cable), Wall panel choices include acoustic felt back wall, whiteboard, wall panels, photography on any of the panels, Customized logo and Low-profile walls (no casters)

Micro Plaza

The company also offers a Micro plaza that is a lightweight space that is easy to tear down and move. One can customize it in whatever color one wants and has the feature of magnetic whiteboard trim on sidewalls. It dampens the sounds and could be easily sprayed and disinfected. 

The space table feature has a wall-mounted desk that transforms 2 feet of wall space into an efficient workspace. It could be installed at a sit or stand height with zero Footprint collection that fits right under a standard window with two electrical outlets & two USB chargers. Furthermore, Due to Covid-19, the health professionals have realized the additional health care needs. Keeping this in mind the twofold has introduced the health plaza which has additional capacity and privacy for consultation and care which could be sprayed and disinfected and has visual privacy and lockable medical cooler storage. 

Other options

There are various other options that twofold offers. One of which is a space maker which is perfect for instant space planning having a slim profile with a magnetic whiteboard panel that could be sprayed and disinfected, protection and barrier for new office layouts and separate workstations. The company's space maker collection focuses on providing privacy, sound dampening, and a safe division of open space. Another product is The Twofold Spaces Desk Wrap that has a baseline of the Desk Wrap is PET felt, which acts as a sound dampener, pinboard, and fully cleanable surface, and is available in 20 colors or a custom print. one can easily promote social distancing anywhere in the office with the Space Maker collection.

The company aims to come up with a very new product which is a working wall that is an ultra-compact, on-demand workspace having zero-footprint collection, folds away and one can use the table and seat separately or together along with an optional charging station.
