Stepful | Training You To Become A Certified Medical Assistant

Author

Healthcare is the fastest growing industry in the US accounting for 40% of all jobs added including medical assistants, nursing assistants, and phlebotomists. According to a report by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, job opportunities for medical assistants are expected to grow 18 percent from 2020 to 2030, much faster than the national average for all occupations (4 percent ). Over the next decade, an average of 104,400 openings for medical assistants are expected. These thriving jobs can be found in hospitals, doctor's offices, and other healthcare facilities.

Stepful was founded in 2021, it's based in New York, United States, and funded by Y-combinator. It helps those without college degrees train for and find entry-level healthcare jobs. One can become a certified clinical medical assistant by four months of training program to perform administrative and clinical responsibilities in not only primary care clinics but also in the hospitals. They have a partnership with National Healthcareer Association (NHA). The company is on an ambitious mission to create economic opportunities for one million job seekers in healthcare. Stepful graduates work at Northwell health, Sunrise labs, Davita, and New York-presbyterian.

Training

The training comprises 40-80 clinical hours and also the choice of evening or morning class. One can learn remotely and in-person, part-time, and at their schedule. The company offers tuition with 100% job guarantee. 90% of graduates are employed within three months after graduation. Interestingly, if one doesn't get the job after 6 months of the certification then the company offers a money-back guarantee. Also, students have the options to pay upfront, in installments, or with a loan.

Stepful is partnered with Climb Credit, a student lending company whose mission is to finance only high-quality education. The tuition includes online instructor-led Zoom classes, clinical sessions, an electronic textbook, NHA certification exam prep, dedicated 1:1 coaching, and job placement. Stepful offers the phlebotomy and EKG training programs consisting of online classes and have have one week in-person clinical in Manhattan, New York. One can be nationally certified after obtaining the NHA certification and will be able to work in every US state except California and Washington. 

Phlebotomy

For the phlebotomy course, one can learn how to draw blood and analyze lab samples via instructor-led Zoom classes and then Learn how to find veins, apply tourniquets and assemble tools via in-person clinical sessions. They offer students to practice drawing blood samples from mannequins and partners for better skills. Not only this, Stepful makes sure the candidate is well prepared for the national exam.

Furthermore, students can work with one dedicated coach to find a healthcare job that matches one's preferences. For the EKG course, one can Learn patient care foundations and master EKG acquisition, analysis, and interpretation. The Topics covered include obtaining vital signs, performing rapid response procedures, and EKG testing. Also, students can practice operating an EKG machine and interpreting patient results.

One can also get the opportunity to hire reliable, vetted phlebotomists, EKG technicians, and medical assistants from the stepful's platform by accessing an exclusive pipeline of diverse, qualified candidates and filling the healthcare vacancies quickly. The stepful graduates are certified by National Healthcareer Association and vetted for professionalism, reliability, and grit.
