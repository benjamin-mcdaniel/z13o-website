The 10 Must-Follow Marketing Tips before Launching an App

Author

You have reached the stage where your app is finished developing and is prepared to be released on the market. You've put a lot of effort into getting here, so take some time to figure out what to do before, during, and after your mobile app launch. In this article, we will share some important tips that will assist you in your pre-launching strategy. 

These 10 Marketing tips will help you in your pre-launching strategy

1) Study your competition

A good sports team spends time researching their opponent before any important game. Research on each opponent's player will be done by coaches, who will also view the game tape and compile scouting reports. A competition analysis is a technique for creating a game plan that will help your business prosper, similar to a scouting report for your industry.

Look for similar apps in the google play store and the apple app store, check them out and note their weaknesses and strengths. This will also help you have a better idea of your app.You still have time to alter features or functionalities. Look at their Reviews and Ratings to identify the features that annoy users the most and eliminate them in your app.

It's not about duplicating other apps; it's about making yours as good as you can! Analyze your competitors and profit from their weaknesses!

2) Define your goals and target audience

First and foremost, make sure your product is original before investing your time in designing and developing it. Since there are so many apps available, it can be difficult to outperform your rivals these days. You should have clear goals and a target audience in your mind.

Set realistic goals rather than being overly greedy. You should be targeting your audience as you are identifying and planning your objectives. What features does your product possess, in your opinion? What are its major purposes? Which demographic would benefit from your app or game the most? You should have answers to these questions and market your app accordingly.

3) App Store Optimization

Getting your app noticed is one of the biggest problems facing mobile app publishers today, with more than 6 million mobile apps available in the major app stores. This is why it's so important to understand app store optimization (ASO).

Mobile apps are optimized for app stores to appear better in search results. This is known as app store optimization. Potential users will see your app more often the higher it ranks in the search results of an app store. More people frequently visit the app store page for your app as a result of the enhanced visibility.

Don't forget about optimizing for the app store. Your list of priorities should always include app store optimization. ASO assists you in increasing your app visibility in the store, increasing your conversion rate, and generating more organic installs. Before publishing your app, make sure that all of your ASO components (app store listings) are optimized. This will give you an edge over your competitors.

4) Create a Landing Page

A landing page is a separate web page designed expressly for a marketing or advertising campaign in digital marketing. It's the page that a visitor "lands" on after clicking on an email link, an advertisement from Google, Bing, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, or another comparable website.

Landing pages are created with a single emphasis or purpose, known as a call to action, as opposed to other web pages, which often have multiple aims and encourage exploration.

Make a landing page and send your target audience there. You can use this landing page to explain the app's features and to advertise your app. With WordPress, Wix, or any other content management system for quickly building websites, you can design your landing page. You can also share this landing page on social media websites like Facebook, Reddit, Twitter e.t.c and get free organic users for your app. This is a very important tip. Share your landing page where your target audience hangs out and share your landing page there. 

5) Pay attention to your Content Marketing Strategy

The creation and distribution of relevant, useful content such as blogs, newsletters, white papers, social media postings, emails, videos, and similar itemsâ€”to current and future clients is known as content marketing. When done well, this content demonstrates competence and makes it obvious that a business values the customers it serves.

Using content marketing consistently helps you build and maintain relationships with both current and potential clients. When your target market views your business as a partner invested in their success and a reliable source of information and guidance, they are more likely to choose you when the time comes to make a purchase.

Welcome others to become a part of your community. Try to catch their interest with interesting and compelling content. Create interest in your app and keep it alive by posting interesting content frequently on your blog or social media pages about it.

You need to consider your target audience once more at this point. Consider what they could be looking for and where they might find it. Along with creating your blog, you may also contact some blogs that currently have traffic that relates to your target audience and exchange guest posts. Draw attention to the features and benefits of your app and include links that will direct readers to your landing page.

6) Use Social Media to your advantage

For businesses of all sizes, social media marketing is an effective way to connect with prospects and clients. If you're not using social media sites like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn, you're missing out. Effective social media marketing can help your business achieve extraordinary success by generating loyal supporters, leads, and even purchases.

Start promoting the features and capabilities of your app on various social media platforms. People will be interested in your launch and wait for it because of this. Use the social media channels that your target audience utilizes the most and consistently upload fresh, engaging and unique content.

Sending reminder emails that highlight your launch date can help you generate even more buzz. Try to capture people's interest as much as you can!

7) Create a Video Teaser

Teasers are brief videos with the primary goal of generating buzz. You usually picture teaser videos for movies or songs, however this form of video isn't solely used in the entertainment sector. In reality, teaser videos can play a crucial role in a business's product or service marketing strategy.

Nowadays, the majority of content is created with the audience's wants and needs in mind. Teaser videos are designed to leave viewers hanging. They only provide the audience with enough information to stimulate their interest. People are attracted by effective teaser videos by getting a sneak peek of what will be revealed later. This can give you the upper hand over business rivals that are competing for customers and keep you at the forefront of people's minds.

It's a proven fact that people would rather watch than read. So creating a video is a must. Make a short, straightforward video that highlights the most important features of your app. Grab the attention of the user within the first 5 seconds and you should also include a call to action message. You can use this video to advertise on social media networks. You can create advertisements and specifically target your target audience on social media websites like facebook, reddit e.t.c using the teaser video.

8) Broaden your network

Join online communities related to your niche on social media sites like Facebook, Reddit, Instagram, and LinkedIn and start networking. Networking is very powerful and can help you achieve massive business success. Provide real value to those online communities. tell them how your app solves problems and the main features of your app. 

As soon as you can, start making new friends, and get in touch with media contacts. Social media followers are significantly influenced by influencers. They may spread the word about your app among their following, which would increase the number of individuals eagerly anticipating your launch.

You can also contact the social media influencers and work out some kind of promotion deal with them. They will charge you but it can be worth it in the long run. As mentioned above, these influencers have hundreds of thousands of followers. They are very powerful in the social media space. If they can promote your app in their posts, You will get many installs.

9) Make sure you have a neat app

Before your product's launch, you need input. You have only received input from your team up to this point, but you now need an outside perspective.

Make sure that your app does not have any bugs or glitches. If there are any bugs or glitches, make sure to remove them before launch. If they are not removed then the users will give you bad reviews and ratings on app stores and this can hurt the success of your app. Therefore it is very important for you that you release a clean app, free of bugs and glitches.

Have a few of your target audience members beta-test your app and provide feedback on what should be improved. When your product is eventually launched, be sure to reward them. You can give them access to exclusive content, special features, deals, discounts, or anything else you can think of.

10) Schedule your launching date

We are aware that you would like to release your app on the day that your developers inform you it is complete, but in all honesty, this is not a wise move. You must generate buzz before making your app available in the stores, as we have been advising you throughout this article. Make sure you have enough time to promote your app and make sure the day it launches doesn't coincide with any major industry events.

Are you ready to launch your app?

In this article, We shared the ten most important marketing tips before launching an app. Implement these before you launch your app. You will have amazing results. 

To sum it up the top 10 marketing tips before launching an app are to study your competition, define your goals and target audience, App Store Optimisation (ASO), Create a landing page, Pay attention to your content marketing strategy, use social media to your advantage, create a video teaser, broaden your network, make sure you have a neat app, schedule your launching date.
